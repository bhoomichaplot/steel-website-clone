Production Capacity

Presently the company has facilities and capacity to produce 20,000 M.T. per annum of Cold Rolled and Annealed strips and 12,000 M.T. per annum of Hardened and Tempered strips. Along with the excellent machinery the company is empowered with its dedicated human resource of 260 skilled and professional employees which is a perfect blend of youth knowledge and experience. This has been instrumental in the consistent growth of the company. The plant and machinery is under Autonomous preventive maintenance monitored by Total Productive Assets Management (TPAM) and Quality Circles.
